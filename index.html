Before You Begin

English:
Welcome. Before you begin, please read this carefully. This diagnostic is designed to place you in the right level — the level where you will learn the most and progress the fastest. There are no pass or fail results. Using AI, searching for answers online, or asking for help will only place you in the wrong class. We will discover your real level through your speaking and writing in class. Cheating this diagnostic only cheats yourself. Please answer every question honestly and to the best of your ability. Good luck.

Arabic:
